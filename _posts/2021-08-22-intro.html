High Concept Statement

Our capstone project is a 3rd-person Action game centered around a fluid, multi-weapon combat system. The events of the game will focus on fighting various enemies to collect three swords. The game will culminate in a boss fight where the player will use the skills and abilities they have gathered to defeat their enemies and save their home.

Features

  • A horde of bandits will descend on your town to steal the sacred sword and its associated powers. After a horrific war, you are one of the only remaining able-bodied “adults.” As a result, you’ve been tasked with mastering this blade and fighting them off.
  • In a short tutorial, the player will start with a training sword, learning the controls and mechanics of the game.
  • As you progress, you will unlock three unique swords. Each sword gives you access to new abilities and greater adaptability in combat.
  • Each sword is designed to focus on different aspects of combat: power, speed, and health. However, switching to a different sword will take time, forcing players to consider the optimal sword(s) for each situation.
  • The player will also have the ability to block, dodge, and parry enemy attacks.
  • Throughout the game, there will be three enemy types. One standard enemy and two enemies that are designed around the power and speed swords.
  • After obtaining all three swords, the player will end up back at their town to face off against the bandit leader.
