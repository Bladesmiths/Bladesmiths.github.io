About our game

This Year's Sword Game (TYSG) is a 3rd-person action game centered around a real-time weapon-switching combat system.

You take up this blade to slash, roll, and parry your way through a horde of bandits who are attempting to invade your town and take the sword for themselves.

Once you've gathered more gems and mastered your weapon, the game culminates in a boss fight against the fearsome bandit leader, who wields a sacred sword of their own.
