The Shattering Swords (TSS) is a 3rd-person action game centered around a real-time weapon-switching combat system.

Combat is Dark-Souls-style but you have a magical sword that can change its shape on the fly when exposed to various gemstones.

You play as a reluctant hero who takes up the sword to defend their village from a horde of bandits.

You'll slash, roll, and parry your way through combat encounters, gathering new gemstones and mastering your weapon.

The game culminates in a boss fight against the fearsome bandit leader, who wields a sword that mirrors your own.
